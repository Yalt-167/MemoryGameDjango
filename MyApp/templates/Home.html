Welcome to the Memory Game!





Memory Game is a classic card matching game where the goal is to find all matching pairs of cards.
Here's how to play:


    1. Click on a card to reveal its content.

    2. Click on another card to try to find its matching pair.

    3. If the two cards match, they will remain open. If not, they will flip back.

    4. Continue until all pairs are matched.

The game requires concentration and a good memory to succeed. Enjoy playing and have fun!
